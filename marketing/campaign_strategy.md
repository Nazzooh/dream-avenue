# Campaign & Lead Generation Strategy

## 1. Campaign Ideas

### A. "The Dream Wedding Showcase" (Seasonal)
*   **Concept:** A virtual or physical open house showcasing the venue set up for a grand wedding.
*   **Content:** High-quality video tours, testimonials from past couples, behind-the-scenes showing the "making of" a luxury event.
*   **Goal:** Drive bookings for the upcoming wedding season.

### B. "Corporate Excellence Series"
*   **Concept:** Position Dream Avenue as the hub for serious business and networking.
*   **Content:** Photos of conference setups, high-tech amenities (screens, sound systems), and professional catering options.
*   **Goal:** Attract corporate clients for seminars, product launches, and annual meets.

### C. "Monsoon Magic" (Off-Season Push)
*   **Concept:** Highlight the beauty of Calicut in the rain and how Dream Avenue provides a cozy, luxurious indoor shelter for events.
*   **Goal:** Increase bookings during the typically slower monsoon months.

## 2. Lead Generation Strategies

### A. Social Media Ads (Meta & Instagram)
*   **Targeting:** Engaged couples in Calicut/Kerala (Age 23-35), HR Managers, Event Planners.
*   **Lead Magnet:** "Download our Exclusive Wedding Package Guide" or "Get a Virtual Tour Link" in exchange for email/phone.

### B. SEO & Content Marketing
*   **Blog Posts:** "Top 10 Wedding Trends in Kerala for 2026", "How to Plan a Corporate Event in Calicut".
*   **Local SEO:** Ensure Google My Business is fully optimized with new photos (like the ones generated) and positive reviews.

### C. Strategic Partnerships
*   **Vendors:** Partner with top wedding planners, photographers, and caterers in Calicut. Referral programs where they recommend Dream Avenue.

### D. Website Optimization
*   **Pop-up Offer:** "Planning an event? Get a free consultation and quote today."
*   **Retargeting:** Run ads to users who visited the 'Booking' or 'Packages' page but didn't convert.
